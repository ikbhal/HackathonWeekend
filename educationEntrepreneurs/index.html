A Global Movement to Improve Life

Hackathon Weekend was founded on the belife that Hackathon is a powerful force to solve problems. We are a global initiative that presents unique opportunities for anyone interested in solving problem, can participate in Hackathon. We started in Bangalore. We are planning to conduct Hackathon Weekend in every City. Hackathon is great place to meet all kind of people from beginner to expert from different background to solve problem of yours or others for betterment of life.

Friday - Online

Pitch your idea, vote for your favorites, form teams, and get to work. Not pitching? Not a problem. Someone needs you on their team!

Saturday - Build

Work with your team to further solve problem and try to come up a complete one case solution. Work. Eat. Talk And repeat.

Sunday - Online Launch

It’s showtime! Put final touches on your deck and present your case to the Judges. Show them why you’re the best solution and how it impact others.
